The Grommet is the birthplace of Citizen Commerce™.

We believe every purchase amplifies something in the world, good, bad or indifferent. At the Grommet we’re creating a place for people who want their purchases to have meaning – to support what matters most to them: whether it’s technical innovation, green or social enterprises, the creation of jobs, domestic manufacturing, or the preservation of craft.

And we’re rethinking the way people fundamentally launch, discover, share, influence and buy these kinds of products online. Given that our consumer purchases represent 65% of the US economy, that’s a pretty powerful place to start changing the world.
